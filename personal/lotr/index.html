One Ring Poems Collection

I am a huge fan of J.R.R. Tolkien's works. I once read the One Ring poem in Japanese and after that wondered how it might look in other languages. After that I tried searching other versions but there are a lot of different old websites, that either use images, or have some languages that I can't even pronounce and they didn't add phonetics to help reading them. So I wanted to make a more complete website with all kind of details.

One Ring

Three Rings for the Elven-kings under the sky,

Seven for the Dwarf-lords in their halls of stone,

Nine for Mortal Men doomed to die,

One for the Dark Lord on his dark throne

In the Land of Mordor where the Shadows lie.

One Ring to rule them all, One Ring to find them,

One Ring to bring them all and in the darkness bind them

In the Land of Mordor where the Shadows lie.

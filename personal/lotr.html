This is a website I made to have the verse of the One Ring in as many languages as I could find.

I guess there are already other people who had this idea, but since I couldn't find anything.

I'll just post here the translations I know or I can find from the books in different versions.

I hope you like it, and if you want to collaborate you can email me at: visama396 @ gmail.com

English

Three Rings for the Elven-Kings under the sky,

Seven for the Dwarf-Lords in their halls of stone,

Nine for Mortal Men doomed to die,

One for the Dark Lord on his dark throne

In the Land of Mordor where the shadows lie.

One Ring to rule them all, One Ring to find them,

One Ring to bring them all and in the shadows bind them

In the Land of Mordor where the shadows lie.
